Installing Your Modem

Congratulations for buying the SuperUltra VoiceModem 56000+ ! In this section we will show you how to install your modem, and configure it for use with popular telecommunications software.

NOTE:
Before installing the modem you should ground yourself by touching a kitchen sink, radiator or metal plumming. Failing to do som may cause damage to your modem.
  1. Turn off your computer and unplug it from the electrical outlet.
  2. Remove the screws from your computer's cover and then remove the cover. Refer to your computer manufacturer's manual if you need further
    instructions.
  3. Locate an empty expansion slot. Carefully insert the modem into the slot.
  4. Connect the phone cord connected to the wall to the phone jack connector (see the figure)
  5. Connect your receiver to the receiver jack
  6. Connect the external voice source to the line in jack
  7. Connect the line out jack to your hifi amplifier.
  8. Put the cover back on, connect the power and turn on your computer
